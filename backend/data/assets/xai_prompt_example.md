# xAI Prompt Example for Kid-Friendly Dobble Cards

Generate a single colorful cartoon illustration of one iconic wizarding character from the Harry Potter universe. The image should be designed for children and work well on a Dobble card.

- Style: bright pastel colors, simple and friendly children’s book style
- Character: magical school student, witch, or wizard inspired by Harry Potter
- Features: large friendly eyes, round glasses, a small lightning-style forehead mark, robe, wand, and a cheerful expression
- Background: clean white background, no text or logos
- Mood: playful, kid-safe, cute, and easy to recognize
- Add magical elements and creatures from the movies: owls, broomsticks, cauldrons, potion bottles, spell books, house scarves, golden snitches, enchanted hats, and friendly magical animals like owls, hippogriffs, nifflers, and unicorns
- Also include magical items and movie details: wizard hats, wands, spell scrolls, floating candles, potion vials, glasses, and mystical sparkles

This illustration will be used on a children’s Dobble card, so keep the design simple, readable, and fun for young players.
